Seven Must-Visit Famous Landmarks in Tokyo

Tokyo, as Japan's capital, is a city that combines the old with the new. From ancient temples to modern skyscrapers, it offers something for everyone. Whether you're a history buff, a tech enthusiast, or simply looking for a place to relax, these seven landmarks are must-visits for anyone exploring Tokyo.

Tokyo Tower

Location: Minato

Address: 4 Chome-2-8 Shibakoen, Minato City, Tokyo 105-0011

Phone: +81 3-3433-5111

Website: tokyotower.co.jp

One of Tokyo’s most iconic landmarks, Tokyo Tower offers breathtaking views of the city and is a symbol of Japan’s post-war recovery.

Roppongi Hills

Location: Minato

Address: 6 Chome-10-1 Roppongi, Minato City, Tokyo 106-6108

Phone: +81 3-6406-6000

Website: roppongihills.com

A bustling urban center known for its upscale shopping, art museums, and stunning views from the Roppongi Hills Mori Tower.

Meiji Shrine

Location: Shibuya

Address: 1-1 Yoyogikamizonocho, Shibuya City, Tokyo 151-8557

Phone: +81 3-3379-5511

Website: meijijingu.or.jp

A serene shrine dedicated to Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken, surrounded by a lush forest.
